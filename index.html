The problem

Reservations arrive faster than your team can process them.

Every booking still passes through human hands — read, re-typed, confirmed, and reconciled across tools that don't talk to each other. It's slow, it's fragile, and it doesn't scale.

Scattered across inboxes

Booking.com, Airbnb and direct emails all land in different places, in different formats, at all hours.

Re-typed by hand

Guest names, dates and room types get copied into Excel, sheets or paper — one reservation at a time.

Slow confirmations

Manual calls and replies delay guests and tie up staff who should be running the property.

Costly mistakes

Typos, missed emails and overlapping stays turn into double bookings and unhappy guests.

No single source of truth

Calendars and spreadsheets drift out of sync, so nobody is quite sure what's actually available.

Hours drained every week

Repetitive admin eats the time you should spend on guests, revenue and growing the business.

The solution

One system that runs your booking operations end to end.

We build an automated pipeline around the tools you already use. It listens for reservations, reads them, organizes them and keeps your availability perfectly in sync — completely hands-off.

  • Detects booking emails automaticallyThe second a reservation arrives from any channel.
  • Extracts every detail instantlyGuest, dates, room type, price and channel — structured, not scraped by hand.
  • Stores data in a structured systemClean rows in Google Sheets or your database — formatted and deduplicated.
  • Assigns rooms & availabilityInventory updates automatically, so two guests never get the same room.
  • Syncs your calendar in real timeYour team sees an accurate, up-to-the-minute picture at all times.
  • Eliminates manual entry completelyNo copy-paste, no spreadsheets by hand, no missed reservations.

How it works

From inbox to confirmed — in seconds, without you.

Five automated steps replace an entire manual workflow. Once it's set up, it simply runs.

01

Detect

A reservation lands from Booking.com, Airbnb or direct email. The system catches it instantly, 24/7.

02

Extract

Guest name, dates, room type, price and channel are read and structured automatically.

03

Organize

Clean data flows into your Google Sheet or database — formatted, deduplicated, ready to use.

04

Assign

Rooms and availability update automatically. Overlaps are flagged before they become double bookings.

05

Sync

Your calendar reflects the booking in real time, ready for your team to act on.
